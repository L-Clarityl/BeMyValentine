FFLEWIS

The Origin Story

Once upon a time... long, long ago...(a while back...(like over a year...))

there lived a girl called Fflur and a boy called Lewis

(whom we shall refer hereafter as Lew).

And so...

Upon being overwhelmed by her striking beauty and evident Welsh heritage,

Lew took to her DMs in search of potential love...

It was a journey of great anticipation.

Upon arrival at the mighty gates (opening the iOS keyboard),

Lew mustered up the courage to speak. And what he uttered next

changed the course of Lew and Fflur's history forever...

"Very random question but I have to ask: are you welsh at all"

"That double L in your name might be a give away"

Now, attentive reader, if you have but an eigth of the intelligence

of a plastic cup, you may have noticed that Lew had made a terrible mistake.

In the moment, it sort of felt like this:

But all was not lost. Lew had made it this far and was not willing

to admit defeat. And so, Fflur and Lew begin chirping like birds on a

spring afternoon (texting a lot on iMessage), engaging in thrilling rounds

of virtual 8 ball, selectively missing classes to watch Traitors, and the like...

A few months later...

Now the 27th of January, Fflur and Lew find themselves in an awkwardly

romantic situation, as though fate were revealed through some strange

foreshadowing...

The bright pink, the ambient lighting, the sensual music...

It was all too allusive.

Despite all odds, however, Fflur and Lew heartily enjoyed their meal.

A meal forever to be remembered...

Months later...

Lew confesses his feelings, in such a heartwarming way it is

no wonder Fflur reciprocated. The rough translation is as follows:

"Don't deep this, but I think I might be slightly like maybe beginning

to sort of maybe kinda potentially like you."

It's the confession a girl like Fflur girl could only dream of, probably.

And with that, Fflewis was born!
